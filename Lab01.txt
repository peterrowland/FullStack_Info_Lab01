1. Change Directory: cd <directoryName>
2. Create Directory: mkdir <directoryName>
3. Rename Directory: mv <oldname> <newname>
4. List contents: ls <directoryName>
5. Move File: mv <filename> </path/to/location>
6. Copy File: cp <filename> </path/to/location>
7. Search: grep <string> <filename>
8. Access Shell: ssh user@servername
9. remove directory: rmdir <directory> or rm -rf <directory>
